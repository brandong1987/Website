Our Story

studioMaker was born from a simple observation: too many great local businesses were losing customers because of outdated, slow, or non-existent websites. We saw talented contractors, passionate restaurant owners, and skilled service providers struggling to compete online, not because they lacked quality, but because they lacked a quality web presence.

We started studioMaker to change that. Our founder, a web developer with over a decade of experience building everything from enterprise platforms to simple landing pages, realized that local businesses needed a different approach than what traditional agencies were offering. They needed fast turnarounds, transparent pricing, and websites that actually converted visitors into customers.

Today, we've helped over 750 local businesses establish a strong online presence. Every site we build is designed with one goal in mind: to grow your business.
